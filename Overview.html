Web Accessibility: Impact and Benefits

This page will contain short examples of web accessibility features. Each example will be in text, accompanied by a short video, and with links to further reading and resources (eg. Getting Started Tips). Each example shows why a particular accessibility feature - such as video captions - is essential to people with disabilities, and the broader benefits it has for many more. The examples should be compelling and convincing that accessibility is important for your own website, not only "for the sake of these others".
